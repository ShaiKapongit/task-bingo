Task Bingo

What will you get done? Clear a row, column, or diagonal.

How to play

Add tasks to fill a board. The board sizes itself to fit, padding the rest with free cells.

Tap a task when you finish it — it gets crossed off and its time is recorded.

Bingo: complete a full row, column, or diagonal where most of the cells are real tasks (not free spaces).

Small boards (3×3): a bingo line just gives a little flash and your done tasks stay put. The full celebration plays when you finish the whole board.

Bigger boards: a bingo clears those tasks, celebrates, and reshuffles the rest.

Categories: switch to “A board per category” to give each category its own colored card. In “One board”, a colored stripe shows each task’s category.

Free cells: tap one to drop in a new task on the spot.

Edit a task: long-press any task to change its text or category, mark it done, add subtasks, or delete it. A task can also have no category.

Subtasks: give a task smaller steps (e.g. "Read the series" → Book 1–4). Little bars on the cell show progress; the task finishes when every subtask is checked, or when you tap the task itself.

Rename: tap the ✎ by the title to name your board whatever you like.

Rewards: in the Rewards section you can list real-world treats to earn. A line bingo wins a Small reward, finishing a whole board wins a Big one, and every 3 boards unlocks a Premium reward. Each can be repeatable or one-time. Earned rewards collect in a list you tap to redeem.

Buttons: Shuffle re-arranges, Tidy merges boards into the fewest, Clear all starts fresh. Finished and Stats live at the bottom.

Tip: separate multiple tasks with commas (or new lines) to add several at once — they'll all use the category below.

To add
